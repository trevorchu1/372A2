Travelogue: Iceland

Overview

Iceland enchants visitors with its timeless beauty and vibrant culture. Breathtaking mountains, powerful waterfalls, and exotic wildlife are just some of the wonders you'll see in Iceland!

Must-See Spots

  1. Reykjavik
  2. Blue Lagoon
  3. Westfjords
  4. Akureyri
  5. Lava Fields

Visitor Tips

  • Food is considerably more expensive than in America, budget accordingly!
  • Bring waterproof layers of clothing!
  • Plan tours outside of the cities in advance!
  • Try the famous hot dog carts in Reykjavik!
